# Portfolio CW2 — Final Report Summary

## Business Problem
Predict whether an e-commerce shipment will be 
delivered late or on time.

## Dataset
- Source: Kaggle (E-Commerce Shipping Dataset)
- Records: 10,999 rows, 12 columns

## Key Findings
- 60% of orders are delivered late
- Discount offered is the strongest predictor
- Package weight affects delivery timing
- K-Means clustering identified 3 customer segments

## Model Performance
| Model | Accuracy |
|-------|----------|
| Logistic Regression | ~67% |
| Random Forest | ~69% |
